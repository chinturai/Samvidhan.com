About this course

The Executive course simplifies the structure and functioning of the Indian government's executive branch. Learners will explore the roles, powers, and responsibilities of key offices, including the President, Prime Minister, Governors, and the Council of Ministers. The course covers the distinction between the Union and State Executives, focusing on how they work together to govern the country. Key topics include the election and appointment processes, the concept of collective responsibility, and the executiveâ€™s role in policy implementation. It also addresses emergency powers, recent challenges, and reforms aimed at improving governance and accountability within the executive system.
