About this course

The Judiciary course provides a simplified overview of the Indian judicial system, focusing on its structure, powers, and functions. Learners will gain insights into the hierarchy of courts, including the Supreme Court, High Courts, and Subordinate Courts, and their roles in upholding the rule of law. The course explains the judicial process, the importance of judicial review, and the protection of fundamental rights. Key topics include the appointment and removal of judges, landmark judgments, and Public Interest Litigation (PIL). The course also explores challenges like case backlogs and recent reforms aimed at ensuring efficient judicial functioning.
