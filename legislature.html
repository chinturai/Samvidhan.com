About this course

The Legislature course provides a comprehensive yet simplified understanding of the Indian parliamentary system, focusing on its bicameral structure comprising the Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha. It covers the roles, powers, and responsibilities of each house, along with the detailed lawmaking process, explaining how bills are introduced, debated, and passed. Learners will explore the election process of members, the significance of parliamentary committees, and the role of the Speaker and Chairman. The course also addresses key legislative challenges like disruptions, inefficiencies, and the need for reforms, offering insights into recent developments aimed at improving legislative functioning and governance in India.
